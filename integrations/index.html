FAQ

Common questions

Anything else? Email support@edgeiqlabs.com

Does this work with Slack's free plan?
Yes. Slack's incoming webhooks are available on all plans including free. Just create an incoming webhook in your Slack app settings and paste the URL.
What does the JSON payload look like?
Every alert includes: {"type": "ssl_expiry", "domain": "example.com", "severity": "warning", "message": "...", "timestamp": "...", "details": {...}}. Consistent across all alert types so your automation can handle them uniformly.
Can I send alerts to multiple destinations?
Yes. Pro supports up to 3 webhook destinations. You can send to Slack, Discord, and a custom endpoint simultaneously.
Which EdgeIQ tools send webhooks?
All of them: SSL Watcher (expiry alerts), Vendor Watch (outage + all-clear), Pulse (daily risk digest), and Inbox Shield (email security changes). New tools automatically wire in.
What happens if my endpoint is down?
EdgeIQ retries the delivery up to 3 times with exponential backoff (1 min, 5 min, 15 min). If all retries fail, you'll receive an email fallback instead.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No. Month-to-month only. Cancel from your Stripe billing portal any time — no questions, no friction.
