Questions

Do you need access to my server or credentials?

No. All scans are read-only and run against your public-facing infrastructure — the same checks any attacker could run from the internet. Nothing is installed on your servers.

What does "change detection" mean?

We store the results of each scan and compare them to the previous week. If your subdomain count goes from 4 to 5, or your SSL expires in under 30 days, or a new CVE matches your server software — you get an alert. You won't get emails when nothing changes.

Can I monitor multiple domains?

The free plan covers 1 domain. Pro covers 5, Business covers 20. Submit separate sign-up forms for additional domains on the free plan — one domain per registration.

How do I cancel?

Email support@edgeiqlabs.com and we'll remove your domain from the monitoring queue immediately. For paid plans, you can also cancel through your Stripe customer portal.

Is this similar to Intruder or Detectify?

EdgeIQ Pulse does roughly what Intruder's basic tier does — scheduled scans + email alerts — at a fraction of the price. Intruder starts at $113/mo. Our goal is to make that level of coverage accessible to businesses under 50 people.
