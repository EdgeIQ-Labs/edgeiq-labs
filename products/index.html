How Fix-it Works

A simple 4-step flow that turns findings into action:

  1. Detect: EdgeIQ scanners identify issues (SSL, headers, endpoint risk, etc.).
  2. Classify: Findings are scored by severity so urgent items are prioritized first.
  3. Remediate: Fix-it runs approved auto-actions (SSL renew, header hardening, issue creation).
  4. Verify: Re-check confirms the fix and logs before/after evidence.
