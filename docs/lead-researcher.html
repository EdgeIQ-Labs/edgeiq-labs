Lead Researcher

Enrich company/domain leads with tech stack and business context.

Setup

curl -sL https://edgeiqlabs.com/dl/edgeiq-lead-researcher.py -o edgeiq-lead-researcher.py
python3 edgeiq-lead-researcher.py --domain example.com --out lead.json

How to Use

  1. Run lookup by domain or company name.
  2. Validate tech-stack and contact signals.
  3. Export CSV/JSON to CRM pipeline.

Troubleshooting

  • Sparse output: retry with company + domain together.
  • Stale data: refresh weekly and diff changes.
