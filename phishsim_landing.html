Questions

Is this legal? Can I send phishing emails to my own employees?

Yes — authorized phishing simulation is a standard security practice used by enterprises worldwide. You must have authorization from your organization to run simulations against your own employees. EdgeIQ PhishSim is designed for authorized internal use only. Never use it against people or organizations without explicit written authorization.

Do I need to set anything up on my mail server?

No. Campaigns send from EdgeIQ's sending infrastructure via Amazon SES. You don't need to touch your own mail server, configure SPF records, or whitelist any IPs. Just upload your target list and send.

How do I access my GoPhish dashboard?

After subscribing you'll receive your customer ID on the welcome page. Visit edgeiqlabs.com/phishsim/, enter your customer ID, and you'll be connected directly to your dedicated GoPhish instance. Bookmark it for easy access.

How many people can I target per campaign?

There's no hard cap on targets per campaign, but for best deliverability we recommend keeping individual campaigns under 500 recipients. Run multiple campaigns or use staggered scheduling for larger teams.

What happens when someone clicks the phishing link?

They're shown a realistic credential harvest page. If they submit, their name is logged. Either way, the click is tracked and they're automatically assigned a training module to complete. You'll see the data in your dashboard in real time.

How do I cancel?

Email support@edgeiqlabs.com and we'll cancel your subscription immediately. You can also cancel through your Stripe billing portal. No cancellation fees or lock-in periods.
